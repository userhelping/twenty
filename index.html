We Are Ducks

Ducks dive into learning and research, looking for answers to some of humanity’s biggest questions.

We're passionate about nature and technology, music and science, abstract art and discrete mathematics, business innovation and human service.

We believe in respect and inclusion, open conversations, and rewarding collaborations.

We work hard, play productively, and pursue life-changing knowledge in one of our planet's most beautiful places.
